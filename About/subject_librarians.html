Each department/discipline has a subject librarian who serves as their expert liaison.

Faculty and students may contact their subject librarian directly with questions about conducting research or developing the library's collection in that field.

Online appointment form links are available for some librarians. If not, please feel free to email your librarian to schedule an appointment. If your librarian has the online appointment form but does not have an appointment slot available at a time you can make, please email them as well.
