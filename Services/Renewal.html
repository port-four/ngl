SHSU Students/Faculty/Staff

  • Visit My Account in the Library's catalog to renew your books.
  • You may also renew books in person at the Library or by calling 936-294-1618.

Who Can Renew Online

  • Students, faculty, and staff with fewer than 15 books overdue and less than $10 in late fines.
  • The system may block online renewal for 15+ books overdue or $10+ in late fines (including currently accruing/estimated fines on overdue items).

Having Problems Renewing Online?

  • Use the online form for TexShare or Courtesy Card Holders to request renewal
  • Or call the Service Desk at 936-294-1618
  • Or email the Service Desk at lib_circ@shsu.edu.

Borrowing and renewal policies: see the Library's Circulation Policies.
